Welcome to my personal website! I'm Ibon Martínez-Arranz, a Data Science expert specializing in Machine Learning and statistical analysis.




Hi there! I'm Ibon Martínez-Arranz, a math and statistics enthusiast with a journey that started at the University of the Basque Country (EHU/UPV). After earning my BSc in Mathematics, I expanded my expertise with two MSc degrees in Applied Statistics Techniques and Mathematical Modeling.

In 2010, I joined OWL Metabolomics and currently lead the Data Science Department. My role involves extensive work in machine learning, managing predictive models, and statistical analysis.

This site showcases my CV and personal projects, highlighting my experience in data science, machine learning, genetic algorithms, Python, and R. Welcome, and I hope you enjoy exploring my work and projects!
